Your Hair Replacement Questions Answered

Common questions about our non-surgical hair replacement systems.

With proper care and maintenance, a hair system can last anywhere from 6 months to over a year. We provide a full maintenance schedule and the best products to maximize the lifespan of your system. Factors like your lifestyle, activity level, and how well you follow our care instructions will influence the longevity.

Absolutely. Our systems are bonded with a medical-grade adhesive that is waterproof and secure, allowing you to continue all your normal activities, including swimming, working out, and showering, with complete confidence. We recommend using a swimming cap for prolonged exposure to chlorinated water and proper cleaning afterward.

The base of our systems is a micro-thin membrane that bonds to your scalp and feels like a second skin. It is virtually imperceptible to the touch, so you (and others) will only feel a full head of hair. The hair itself is 100% human hair, so it has the same texture and feel as natural hair.

We recommend professional maintenance every 4-6 weeks to keep your system looking its best. This includes cleaning, reattachment, and any necessary styling adjustments. Between visits, daily care is minimal - similar to caring for your natural hair but with specialized products we provide. We'll train you on all necessary maintenance during your fitting.
