Wig & Hairpiece FAQs

Common questions about our luxury wigs and hairpieces.

Our primary goal is a natural, undetectable result. We use high-quality materials and provide professional custom fitting and styling. A well-fitted, properly styled wig from our collection will look and move just like natural hair. We pay special attention to details like the hairline, parting, and overall shape to ensure a completely natural appearance.

We provide detailed instructions and recommend specific products for washing and styling your wig or hairpiece. Proper care is essential to maintain its appearance and longevity. Human hair wigs require more styling but can be treated much like natural hair, while synthetic wigs hold their style with minimal effort but require specialized cleaning products. We offer aftercare workshops and maintenance services to help you keep your wig in perfect condition.

Absolutely. All our consultations and fittings are conducted in a private, comfortable room. We understand the sensitive nature of hair loss and are committed to ensuring your experience is discreet and supportive. Many of our team members have personal experience with hair loss, so we approach every consultation with empathy and understanding.

With proper care, a high-quality human hair wig can last 1-2 years, while synthetic wigs typically last 4-6 months with regular wear. The lifespan depends on factors like how often you wear it, your styling habits, and how well you maintain it. We recommend having a second wig to alternate between, which can extend the life of both pieces. Our maintenance services can also help refresh and restore wigs to extend their lifespan.
