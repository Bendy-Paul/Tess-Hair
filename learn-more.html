PRP Hair Restoration

Harness the power of your own body to restore hair naturally. PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy stimulates dormant follicles and improves scalp health using your body's natural growth factors.

How It Works:

  1. A small amount of your blood is drawn (similar to a routine blood test)
  2. Blood is processed in a centrifuge to separate platelet-rich plasma
  3. Growth factor-rich plasma is carefully extracted
  4. Targeted micro-injections deliver plasma to thinning areas
  5. Growth factors stimulate hair follicles and promote regrowth

Treatment Benefits:

  • Non-surgical and completely safe (uses your own blood)
  • Minimal downtime - resume normal activities immediately
  • Effective for both men and women with thinning hair
  • Stimulates natural hair growth rather than masking the problem
  • Improves hair thickness, strength, and overall quality
  • Can be combined with other treatments for enhanced results
