Our Extension Methods

Choose the perfect application method for your lifestyle and hair type.

Most Popular
Tape-In Extensions

Ultra-thin, lightweight extensions that lie flat against the head for a seamless, natural look. Quick application and removal.

  • Lightweight and comfortable
  • Quick 45-60 minute application
  • Lays flat for invisible results
  • Lasts 6-8 weeks between maintenance
  • Reusable for multiple applications
Starting from £250
Micro-Bead Extensions

Individual strands attached with tiny, silicone-lined beads that grip your natural hair without heat, glue, or tape.

  • No heat, glue, or tape required
  • Minimal damage to natural hair
  • Easy to move up as hair grows
  • Perfect for fine or thin hair
  • Lasts 3-4 months with maintenance
Starting from £350
Luxury
Fusion/Keratin Extensions

Individual strands bonded to your natural hair with keratin protein for the most secure and long-lasting attachment method.

  • Most secure attachment method
  • Moves and feels like natural hair
  • Lasts 3-4 months between maintenance
  • Ideal for active lifestyles
  • Minimal visible attachment points
Starting from £450

Hair Extension FAQs

Common questions about our premium hair extension services.

With proper care, our hair extensions can last 3-6 months before needing replacement. Tape-in extensions typically last 6-8 weeks between maintenance appointments, while micro-bead and fusion extensions can last 3-4 months with proper maintenance. The lifespan also depends on your hair's growth rate and how well you follow our aftercare instructions.

Yes! Our 100% human hair extensions can be styled just like your natural hair. You can use heat styling tools, wash, condition, and even color them (though we recommend professional coloring). We'll provide you with specific care instructions and recommended products to maintain the quality and appearance of your extensions. Avoid applying direct heat to the attachment points to prolong their lifespan.

When applied and maintained correctly by our trained professionals, hair extensions should not damage your natural hair. We use methods that minimize stress on your hair follicles and ensure proper weight distribution. Following our aftercare instructions and attending regular maintenance appointments are crucial for keeping your natural hair healthy while wearing extensions.

During your consultation, we'll assess your hair type, texture, lifestyle, and desired outcome to recommend the best extension method for you. Factors we consider include your natural hair thickness, how active you are, your styling preferences, and your maintenance commitment. We'll explain the pros and cons of each method and help you make an informed decision that aligns with your goals and lifestyle.
