Home Care Questions

Common questions about our professional home care products.

While many commercial products are available, our professional-grade products contain higher concentrations of active ingredients and are specifically chosen to work in synergy with your treatment plan. They are free from harsh chemicals that can strip the hair and irritate the scalp, and are formulated to address specific hair and scalp conditions with proven efficacy.

Our trichologist will recommend a personalized home care regimen for you based on your specific scalp and hair condition identified during your consultation. This ensures you are using the most effective products for your needs. We consider factors like your scalp type, hair texture, specific concerns (such as thinning, oiliness, or sensitivity), and your treatment goals to create a customized product plan.

Yes, some of our general wellness and maintenance products are available for direct purchase. However, for our most targeted and potent therapeutic products, a consultation is required to ensure they are appropriate for your condition. This ensures you get the best results and avoid using products that might not be suitable for your specific hair and scalp needs.

Most clients notice improvements in hair texture and scalp condition within 2-4 weeks of consistent use. However, significant changes to hair growth and density typically take 3-6 months, as this aligns with the natural hair growth cycle. Consistency is key, and we recommend following the usage instructions provided with your personalized product plan for optimal results.
