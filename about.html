About Us

Tess Hair & Scalp Clinic takes pride in delivering exceptional service and ensuring every client leaves completely satisfied. We are dedicated to providing high-quality treatments and elite customer care, helping you achieve healthy, beautiful hair from the roots up. Our specialized scalp and hair treatments are designed to rejuvenate, repair, and restore the natural balance of your hair and scalp. Whether you’re dealing with hair loss, dandruff, scalp irritation, or simply want to enhance your hair’s health and shine, our team of qualified trichology experts is here to guide you every step of the way. At Tess Hair & Scalp Clinic, your confidence begins with healthy hair.

Our Founder

Theresa Anthony, CiRT, ITEC, ART - ( Association of Registered Trichologists)​ ​ Theresa, founder of Tess Hair & Scalp Clinic in Belfast, Ireland, is a certified trichologist who has trained with TrichoCare Education, one of the leading trichology institutions in the U.K, and the first one to offer an accredited trichology course in the U.K. Theresa has been in the hair industry for over 15 years, having trained and worked in Italy, France, and the U.K. She is a master of undetectable hair systems, giving confidence to those affected by hair loss. ​ Theresa is trained and qualified in providing a number of scalp and hair loss treatments, including but not limited to: ​

  • Microneedling, which stimulates the process of wound healing thereby encouraging hair regrowth.
  • Keratolytic scalp treatments for scaly scalp conditions such as seborrhoeic dermatitis, psoriasis, and pityriasis amiantacea.
  • LLLT (low-level laser/light therapy) which has shown great success in some hair loss patients.

Theresa also has a special interest in scalp and hair loss conditions which afflict people with darker skin tones and textured hair and is continuously educating herself on the subject, keeping abreast of the latest research and findings.
